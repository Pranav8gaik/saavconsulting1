About us

A firm built on rigour
and trust.

Saav Consulting is a boutique management consultancy working with mid-market companies and growth-stage businesses across sectors. We help organisations sharpen their strategy, enter new markets, and build capabilities needed to sustain long-term competitive advantage.

Our approach is direct, evidence-based, and deeply collaborative. We don't produce reports that gather dust — we work alongside leadership teams to make strategy real and execution effective.

Senior-led engagements, every time. You work directly with a principal consultant from first conversation to final delivery.

How we work

Principles we
hold to.

01

Senior-led, always.

Every engagement is led by a principal consultant. You will never be handed to a junior team after the proposal stage — the person you meet is the person who does the work.

02

Independence over scale.

We are deliberately small. No hidden upsell agenda — only advice that is genuinely in your interest, free from the conflicts that large firm structures create.

03

Evidence before opinion.

Our recommendations are grounded in data, competitive intelligence, and market reality. We challenge assumptions — including our own — before committing to a direction.

04

Outcomes, not outputs.

We measure our value in business results over time, not in the thickness of reports delivered. Many of our clients return for multiple engagements across years.
